Heart & Bloom Soap Co.

About Heart & Bloom

At Heart & Bloom, we believe self-care should be simple, gentle, and full of heart. Our soaps are handcrafted in small batches using natural ingredients, botanical oils, and scents inspired by the beauty of everyday life.

Our Story

Heart & Bloom began as a small kitchen experiment and quickly blossomed into a passion for crafting soap that feels as good as it looks. What started with a few bars made for friends has grown into a small-batch business dedicated to kindness — to your skin and the planet.

What Matters to Us

  • Pure Ingredients: We use natural oils, clays, and botanicals — never synthetic dyes or harsh chemicals.
  • Eco-Friendly: Our packaging is recyclable, biodegradable, and always plastic-free.
  • Handcrafted: Every bar is made, cut, and wrapped by hand with care and attention to detail.

From Our Hands to Yours

Every soap we make is a reminder that small, thoughtful choices can bring beauty and balance to everyday life.
