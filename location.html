About Heart & Bloom

Heart & Bloom is a small handmade soap company located in the heart of Floral City, FL. We create soaps that are gentle on your skin and kind to the environment.

Our Story

Heart & Bloom began in 2022 as a passion project in a small home kitchen. What started with experimenting with natural oils and botanicals quickly grew into a full-time dedication to creating beautiful, nourishing soaps. Each bar is made by hand with love, care, and a commitment to sustainability.

Our Location

Proudly based in Floral City, FL, surrounded by the natural beauty that inspires our scents and ingredients.

Visit Us

Stop by our workshop to see how our soaps are crafted or find us at local farmers markets and craft fairs.
